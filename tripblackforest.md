# 7-Day Road Trip: Delft to the Black Forest

Here is your complete, updated 7-day road trip itinerary! It includes specific, easy-to-access parking recommendations for every stop so you won't have to stress about where to leave the car.

---

## Day 1: Tuesday, April 7 – Three Countries at Once

* **09:30 AM:** Pick up the rental car in Delft or Rotterdam and start your journey heading southeast.
* **12:30 PM (The Stop):** Arrive at the [Drielandenpunt (Three-Country Point)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/oKvsgM5bbwcCo7ja7) in Vaals.
  > **🅿️ Parking Tip:** Navigate directly to **P1 Three-Country Point** or **Parkeerplaats Drielandenpunt**. These lots are right next to the border monument, meaning very little walking to stand where the Netherlands, Belgium, and Germany meet.
* **01:00 PM:** Have a relaxed lunch at a brasserie on the square. Take the elevator up the Wilhelminatoren watchtower for amazing views without climbing a single stair!
* **02:30 PM:** Hop back in the car and drive into Germany.
* **04:30 PM:** Arrive in Koblenz and check into your hotel. 
  > **🏨 Hotel:** [Trip Inn Hotel Hamm](https://maps.app.goo.gl/RFmGBjAtYi8dgUL86)  
  > **🅿️ Parking:** In hotel
* **05:30 PM:** Take a gentle, completely flat evening stroll to the [Deutsches Eck (German Corner)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/fceDECge9JnuWLjXA) where the Rhine and Moselle rivers meet.

---

## Day 2: Wednesday, April 8 – The Romantic Rhine to Baden-Baden

* **10:00 AM:** Check out and drive south along the left bank of the Rhine River (Route B9) to see the castles on the hills.
* **11:30 AM (The Stop):** Pull over in a historic river town for flat, easy access to the river promenade.
  > **🅿️ Parking Tip:** 
  > * **If you stop in Boppard:** Use **Parkplatz Rheinallee** or **Parkplatz St. Remigiusplatz**. These are right along the water, completely flat, and steps away from cafes.
  > * **If you stop in Bacharach:** Navigate to **Parkplatz Rheinufer Bacharach**. It is perfectly situated right on the banks of the Rhine, meaning you avoid navigating the narrow, medieval streets with the car.
* **02:00 PM:** Arrive in Baden-Baden and check into your hotel.
  > **🏨 Hotel:** [Hotel Bischoff](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Dg2ukjsbShbJTQSs6)  
  > **🅿️ Parking:** [Vincentigarage](https://maps.app.goo.gl/ALGmN2jzJ1RVqMERA)
* **03:30 PM:** Drive to the base of the Merkurbergbahn for the [funicular train](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Yn2boucGAVJ6LUz19). They have a dedicated parking lot right at the bottom station. This funicular train takes you effortlessly to the top of Merkur Mountain for a drink and spectacular views.

---

## Day 3: Thursday, April 9 – The Ultimate Slow Day in Baden-Baden

* **10:30 AM (Morning Stroll):** Take a slow, peaceful walk along the [Lichtentaler Allee](https://maps.app.goo.gl/4WY87L6HiTr3EwCFA). This beautiful park promenade is completely flat.
* **12:30 PM (The Cake!):** Settle into an elegant traditional coffee house (like [Café König](https://maps.app.goo.gl/PKJUGrLQdnZoCxtu5)). Enjoy a light lunch, coffee, and a legendary slice of Schwarzwälder Kirschtorte (Black Forest Cake).
* **02:30 PM (Optional Excursion):** Check energy levels! 
  * *If you are up for it:* Take a short, scenic drive up to [Hohenbaden Old Castle (Altes Schloss)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/sYXpwTZ4hNPaxzog8). You can park for free right at the entrance and see amazing views with almost zero walking.
  * *If you want to relax:* Skip the castle and head back to the hotel for a nap.
* **Evening:** A quiet, relaxed dinner in town.

---

## Day 4: Friday, April 10 – The Scenic Forest Highway to Freiburg

* **10:00 AM:** Leave Baden-Baden and drive onto the B500 (Schwarzwaldhochstraße), one of Germany's most famous scenic routes.
* **11:30 AM (The Stop):** Pull over at [Mummelsee](https://maps.app.goo.gl/jVcPfKa41e9cAW697), a dark, mythical glacial lake.
  > **🅿️ Parking Tip:** There is a large, free parking area directly next to the **Berghotel Mummelsee**, right on the highway called [Parkplatz Mummelsee](https://maps.app.goo.gl/5AZrqP9VeBxehgkG6). Step out of the car, and the lake is right in front of you.
* **12:30 PM:** Enjoy a traditional lunch on the lake's terrace.
* **03:30 PM:** Arrive in Freiburg im Breisgau and check into your hotel. Use the hotel's parking garage.
  > **🏨 Hotel:** [City Hotel Freiburg im Zentrum](https://maps.app.goo.gl/vvrjSpcUMGcPjk4m6)  
  > **🅿️ Parking:** In hotel
* **05:00 PM:** Take a very slow stroll around the [Münsterplatz (the cathedral square)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/B6Fd8voBYfuQ8gik7).

---

## Day 5: Saturday, April 11 – Lake Titisee & Effortless Views

* **10:30 AM:** Take a beautiful 40-minute drive into the deeper forest to [Lake Titisee](https://maps.app.goo.gl/v27UWc1VfPUBq4cV8).
  > **🅿️ Parking Tip:** Use the large, central parking lot called [**Parkplatz Titisee**](https://maps.app.goo.gl/wpGzyw67kuFENYPj7) (near the train station) or the [**Parkplatz an See**](https://maps.app.goo.gl/qBc55hqZjFV5Ry5S8) right at the entrance of the pedestrian zone. It is a very short, flat walk to the water from there.
* **11:30 AM:** Hop on a relaxing, accessible boat cruise on the lake.
* **01:00 PM:** Grab a light lunch from a bakery near the lake, then drive back to Freiburg.
* **03:30 PM:** In Freiburg, take the [Schlossbergbahn](https://maps.app.goo.gl/cy5xsZ25U75bbxJfA). This funicular railway zips you up the mountain in 3 minutes for stunning panoramic views of the city.

---

## Day 6: Sunday, April 12 – Heading North to Heidelberg

* **10:00 AM:** Check out and begin the gentle journey back north.
* **12:00 PM:** Arrive in Heidelberg and check into your hotel.
  > **🏨 Hotel:** [Leonardo Hotel Heidelberg](https://maps.app.goo.gl/GxJ8H1L1FBSCARSG7)  
  > **🅿️ Parking:** In hotel
* **01:30 PM:** Have lunch on the [Hauptstraße](https://maps.app.goo.gl/F18j5d8pdXF1AJXj6), a beautifully flat, pedestrian-only street.
* **03:00 PM:** Visit the famous Heidelberg Castle.
  > **🅿️ Parking Tip:** Park your car in one of the central Altstadt (Old Town) parking garages, like [**P12 Kornmarkt/Schloss**](https://maps.app.goo.gl/pPYkqpjbgn54Sdme7). From P12, you can take the Heidelberger Bergbahn (funicular train) directly up to the castle courtyard without climbing the hill!
* **05:30 PM:** Take the train back down and stroll onto the flat [Alte Brücke (Old Bridge)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/ReRPr4FHxaCFvSJi8) for lovely evening photos.

---

## Day 7: Monday, April 13 – The Relaxed Journey Home

* **09:30 AM:** Have a nice breakfast, pack up the car, and check out.
* **10:30 AM:** Hit the Autobahn heading northwest back toward the Netherlands.
* **01:00 PM:** Stop near the German/Dutch border. Stretch your legs and grab a quick meal at a roadside rest stop.
* **03:30 PM - 04:30 PM:** Arrive back in Delft. Drop off the luggage, return the rental car, and celebrate a wonderful trip!
